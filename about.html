About Us

Our aim is to provide healthcare to all sections of the society and to make health care affordable. The core values at R B Hospital are –clinical expertise, being patient friendly and indulging in ethical practices. The patient forms the centre of all endeavors, patient come first whether it is in the treatment or procedures carried out in outdoor, pathology and indoor treatment. There is thus a strong bond of faith between the patient and the doctor. The patient’s privacy is maintained at all the times. It is the practice to provide the patient all the information in a simple language that is to the point and clear.

At R B Hospital the specialists are highly qualified and experienced, as also compassionate and with a desire to provide the best for patients. They are well complemented by a team of well trained nursing staff and technical staff. The doctors and staff are encouraged to update their skills and expertise through continuing medical education, conferences and publishing their articles and papers.

The infrastructure is of world class standard and the equipment is state of the art.The 3Tesla MRI Multi Transmit & CT Scan 64 Slice machines are the best in UP. There are facilities of modular & linear operation theatres for different specialties. The hospital is equipped with centralized oxygen (Liquid Oxygen Plant), vacuum & compressed air line in different wards. All investigative facilities including 24 hours pathology and blood bank are available. The hospital has a variety of ward facilities starting from general ward to super deluxe suites catering to the requirement of all sections of the society. Apart from that there are 24*7 facilities like chemist shop, ambulance service with wireless networks, 24 ours casualty and emergency services . There are well equipped units to care all types of emergencies and trauma.
